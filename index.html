About Bush & Braai

Building relationships outside the boardroom.

Based in Johannesburg, Bush & Braai is an outdoor experience company that brings professionals together. We believe that some of the best conversations and relationships are built in nature.

By combining physical movement, natural environments, and a traditional braai, we create relaxed spaces where colleagues can connect authentically.

"Our mission is simple: To create meaningful connections between people through nature, movement, and shared experiences."

AM

Ayanda Mahlangu

CEO, Bush & Braai

Why Choose Bush & Braai?

We combine three powerful elements to create a relaxed, memorable, and impactful team-building experience.

Nature

Teams physically disconnect from the high-stress office environment and reconnect with nature, promoting mental wellness and reducing burnout.

Connection

Outdoor settings strip away corporate hierarchy. They naturally break down formal barriers and encourage genuine conversations and stronger relationships.

South African Culture

Nothing brings people together quite like a fire. The traditional braai is the ultimate, uniquely South African way to unwind and socialize.
